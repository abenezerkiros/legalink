About LegalLink 360

Connecting You to Justice, Every Step of the Way

At LegalLink 360, we believe every person deserves access to trusted, effective legal help—especially in life’s most challenging moments. Based in Los Angeles, our mission is simple: to connect accident victims and individuals facing personal injury with compassionate, experienced attorneys who fight for your rights.


Why Choose LegalLink 360?
• Personalized Attorney Matches: We listen to your story and connect you with lawyers who specialize in your type of case—whether it’s a car accident, slip and fall, work injury, or more.
• Bilingual Support: Our team proudly serves both English and Spanish-speaking communities across Los Angeles County.
• No Upfront Costs: Your first consultation is always free, with no obligation. We only work with attorneys who offer “no win, no fee” representation for injury cases.
• Local Knowledge, Real Results: We know the Los Angeles legal landscape—and we use that expertise to get results for our clients.


LegalLink 360 is committed to transparency, rapid response, and relentless advocacy for those who need it most. We handle the intake, screening, and lawyer-matching—so you can focus on healing while we handle the rest.


Start your journey to justice with LegalLink 360. Because when it comes to your future, you deserve a partner who cares.
